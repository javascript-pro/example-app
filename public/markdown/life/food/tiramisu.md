#### Ingredients

- 2 large egg yolks
- 50g caster sugar
- 250g mascarpone
- 120ml strong espresso, cooled
- 5 tbsp Baileys & Terry’s Chocolate Orange Irish Cream Liqueur
- 1 mini panettone (approx. 80–100g)
- Cocoa powder for dusting

[LinkOut title="Baileys & Terry’s Chocolate Orange Irish Cream Liqueur" url="https://www.baileys.com/en-gb/products/baileys-terrys-chocolate-orange-liqueur"]

#### Method

1. Cream base. Whisk yolks and sugar until pale and thick.
   Fold in mascarpone until smooth
2. Coffee mix. Combine espresso and Baileys in a bowl
3. Prepare the panettone. Slice into thin discs or cubes. Give each piece a very quick dip in the coffee mix
4. Assemble

   - Pack a layer of dipped panettone into a small dish  
      (roughly 12–15cm square, or two ramekins)
   - Spread half the mascarpone
   - Add another layer of dipped panettone
   - Finish with the remaining mascarpone

5. Chill. Refrigerate 6 hours or overnight
6. Finish. Dust with cocoa just before serving
